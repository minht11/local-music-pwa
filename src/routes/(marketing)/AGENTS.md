# Marketing Route Guidance

When editing the landing page under `src/routes/(marketing)/`, follow `TONE_OF_VOICE.md`.

Treat it as the source of truth for:

- wording and section tone
- how concrete or technical copy should be
- what kinds of marketing phrases to avoid
- how to keep sections distinct and non-duplicative

Default behavior:

- prefer the clearer version over the cleverer version
- match product terminology exactly when possible
- cut lines that sound generic, cliche, or duplicative
